Our Story

From a late-night dorm-room idea to the busiest spot on campus—this is Campus Bites.

Born From Late-Night Hunger

Campus Bites started in 2018 when three hungry alumni returned to campus and realized students had nowhere to grab a real meal between back-to-back lectures.

What began as a pop-up burger stand outside the library quickly became the Student Union's most beloved eatery. We named our menu items after campus life—the Syllabus Smasher, the Dean's List, Finals Week Espresso—because food should feel as familiar as your favorite study spot.

Today, we serve over 2,000 students and lecturers daily. Our mission is simple: deliver restaurant-quality food at student-friendly prices, with pickup times that respect your schedule. Whether you're cramming for midterms or grabbing lunch between faculty meetings, we've got you covered.

  • 100% locally sourced produce where possible
  • Student workers employed year-round
  • 10% of profits fund campus food insecurity programs
