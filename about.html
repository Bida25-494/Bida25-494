About Click & Eat

Serving the campus community with speed, flavour, and fair prices.

Our Mission

Click & Eat was founded to solve a simple campus problem: long lunch queues and expensive meals that do not fit a student budget. We partner with local suppliers to serve fresh burgers, hotdogs, snacks, and drinks — prepared fast and priced for Botswana's college students and lecturers.

Our mobile-first menu site uses minimal data, so you can browse and order even on a limited bundle. Pick up at our Student Centre kiosk and get back to class on time.

“Tap. Order. Feast.” — that is the Click & Eat promise.

Operating Hours

  • Monday – Friday: 08:00 – 20:00
  • Saturday: 09:00 – 18:00
  • Sunday: 10:00 – 16:00 (exam season extended hours)
  • Public Holidays: Closed (check socials for updates)

Campus Location

Find us at the Student Centre Food Court, Ground Floor, Main Campus — directly opposite the library entrance and next to the ATM plaza.

Look for the orange and black Click & Eat signage at kiosk #3.
