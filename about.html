The Culinary Heartbeat of Campus

Born from a former student's vision — quality food, minutes from the lecture hall.

How Click & Eat Started

During late-night study sessions, our founder — then a final-year engineering student — grew tired of vending-machine snacks and hour-long cafeteria queues. The idea was simple: restaurant-quality burgers and sides, optimised for campus speed.

What began as a pop-up grill beside the library lawn became Click & Eat, a dedicated pickup window serving lecturers, researchers, and thousands of students every semester. We source fresh local ingredients, honour Botswana flavours like phane relish and gemere, and keep every price transparent in Pula.

Today we are more than a food counter — we are where assignments are celebrated, where faculty grab pre-meeting coffee, and where first-years discover their favourite combo. Click. Pick up. Eat.
